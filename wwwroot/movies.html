Interstellar

Interstellar is a film directed by one of the most renowned directors ever, Christopher Nolan. The story follows Matthew McConaughy's character in the distant future living on a dying Earth, he is then recruited to take part in a mission to go find a suitable planet for humans to inhabit once the Earth is no longer habitable. The story takes place across millions of light-years away as well as dealing with the effects of time dilation.
